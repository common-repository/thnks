== Description ==

Thnks is a WooCommerce plugin that allows you to easily customize your thank you page, adding a personal and attractive touch to every purchase made. With Thnks, you can turn every transaction into a more enjoyable experience for your customers. Give your online store the advantage of a personalized thank you with Thnks!

== Installation ==

1- Go to your plugins page on the WordPress dashboard and click on Add New.
2- Click on Upload Plugin and load the Thnks plugin.
3- Once loaded, click on Activate.
4- Go to the Thnks page located in your WordPress dashboard.
5- In the Settings tab, you will find an activation button, click on Activate and then Save.
6- You're all set! You can start using Thnks to customize your thank you pages.
